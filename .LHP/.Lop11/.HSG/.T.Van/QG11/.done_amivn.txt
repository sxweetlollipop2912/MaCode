
PROBLEM: So ban be

Hai so nguyen duoc goi la so ban be neu tong cac uoc so thuc su (ke ca 1) cua so nay
 bang so kia va nguoc lai. 
Chang han, so 220 co cac uoc so la

1 2 4 5 10 11 20 22 44 55 110

Tong cac uoc so tren la 284. 

So 284 co cac uoc so la:

1 2 4 71 142

Tong cac uoc so tren la 220.

Vay 220 va 284 la hai so ban be.

Bai toan: tim tat ca cac so ban be trong mot mien cho truoc
Ten chuong trinh: ami

INPUT:

Cho trong tap tin van ban ami.inp, gom 2 so nguyen L va H tren cung mot dong,
 cach nhau 1 khoang trang (1 <= L <= H <= 900000).

OUTPUT:

Cho trong tap tin van ban ami.out, 
gom 0 hay nhieu dong, moi dong chua 1 cap so nguyen (so nho truoc, so lon sau)
 la so ban be trong khoang L..H. 
Cac cap so duoc sap thu thu theo so dung truoc

Vi du:
ami.inp

200 300


ami.out

220 284
